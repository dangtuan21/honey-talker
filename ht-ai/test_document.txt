University Campus Guide

Welcome to our university campus! This guide contains important information for all students and visitors.

Library Services
The main library is located in the center of campus. It offers:
- Study spaces for individual and group work
- Computer labs with printing services
- Research assistance from librarians
- Access to online databases and journals

Library Hours:
- Monday-Thursday: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM
- Friday: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM  
- Saturday: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- Sunday: 12:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Student Housing
On-campus housing is available for all undergraduate students. Residence halls include:
- Wi-Fi internet access
- Laundry facilities
- Study lounges
- 24/7 security

Dining Services
Multiple dining options are available across campus:
- Main cafeteria (Building A)
- Coffee shop (Library)
- Food court (Student Center)
- Vending machines (all buildings)

Contact Information
For more information, visit the student services office or call (555) 123-4567.
