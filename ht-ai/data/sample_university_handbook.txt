University Student Handbook 2024

ACADEMIC POLICIES

Attendance Policy
Regular attendance is essential for academic success. Students are expected to attend all scheduled classes and laboratory sessions.

- Excused absences require documentation (medical certificate, family emergency, etc.)
- Maximum of 3 unexcused absences per semester before academic warning
- Tardiness beyond 15 minutes counts as absence

Grading System
The university uses a 4.0 grading scale:

- A: 4.0 (Excellent)
- B: 3.0 (Good) 
- C: 2.0 (Satisfactory)
- D: 1.0 (Passing)
- F: 0.0 (Failing)

Academic Integrity
All students must adhere to the highest standards of academic honesty. Plagiarism, cheating, or unauthorized collaboration will result in disciplinary action.

CAMPUS LIFE

Student Organizations
Over 100 student clubs and organizations are available, including:

SAFETY AND SECURITY

Emergency Procedures
In case of emergency:

1. Call campus security: (555) 911-5555
2. Fire alarms: evacuate immediately via nearest exit
3. Medical emergency: call 911 then campus security
4. Severe weather: follow shelter-in-place procedures

Campus Security
24/7 security services include:

- Patrol officers on campus grounds
- Emergency call boxes throughout campus
- ID card access control for buildings
- Security escort service after dark
